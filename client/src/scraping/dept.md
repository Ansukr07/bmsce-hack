## Department Overview  
The Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) department at BMSIT offers a **4-year undergraduate (B.E.) program** with an annual intake of 900 students【47†L610-L616】.  Its vision is: *“To be a centre of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering education and research, nurturing technically competent, ethically responsible, and socially conscious professionals…”*【47†L679-L682】.  The department is NBA-accredited (through June 2025)【47†L641-L647】, reflecting its strong academic quality.  CSE faculty are experienced and research-active (43 with Ph.D., 51 pursuing Ph.D.)【47†L610-L616】, and the department is headed by the Principal (Dr. Satish Kumar T, HoD of CSE).  

## Academics and Programs  
BMSIT CSE offers:  
- **B.E. in Computer Science & Engineering (UG)** – 4-year program, intake 900【47†L610-L616】. The curriculum covers core CS topics (algorithms, databases, networks, AI/ML, etc.) with project-based learning.  
- **M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (PG)** – 2-year VTU-affiliated program (started 2014, intake 18)【49†L600-L608】. This PG program is NBA-accredited (till 2024)【49†L600-L608】 and focuses on advanced areas (networks, security, AI, data science). Students often undertake 8-week industry internships【49†L610-L618】.  
- **M.Tech in Cyber Security (PG)** – 2-year program (started 2022, intake 18)【51†L620-L627】. It is an industry-aligned curriculum (authorized EC-Council academic partner) emphasizing network security, forensics, and modern cyber-defense techniques【51†L620-L628】.  
- **M.Tech in VLSI System Design (PG)** – 2-year program (started 2025–26, intake 24)【53†L622-L627】. This specialization, offered by the ECE department, provides in-depth training in chip design and embedded systems using industry-standard EDA tools【53†L622-L627】【53†L641-L648】.  

These programs are supported by an autonomous VTU framework with regular curriculum updates and industry expert input (e.g. TCS experts for related streams). All courses are taught by Ph.D.-qualified faculty and incorporate lab sessions, workshops, and project work for hands-on experience.  

## Faculty and Staff  
The CSE department has **117 faculty members** (including 43 doctorate holders and 51 Ph.D. scholars) and 16 technical staff【47†L610-L616】. Faculty are actively involved in research publications, conferences, and professional bodies (IEEE, ACM). Many serve on VTU/AICTE academic boards. The team uses modern teaching methods (Outcome-Based Education, project-based learning) and regularly updates course content to match industry trends.

## Facilities and Research  
Key facilities and centers include:  
- **NVIDIA Center of Excellence:** A dedicated lab with GPU servers and AI/ML hardware to support deep learning and graphics projects【47†L644-L649】.  
- **Laboratories:** State-of-the-art computing labs for programming, networking, software engineering, and embedded systems; hardware labs (Microprocessors, Digital Logic, etc.); and high-performance clusters.  
- **Research Centre:** The department maintains a VTU-recognized CSE Research Centre (since 2014)【47†L654-L657】. Faculty and students engage in funded projects (20+ lakhs in consultancy) and have published in indexed journals.  
- **Innovation Spaces:** Access to the campus Idea Lab, Robotics lab, and Incubation Center fosters interdisciplinary projects (IoT, robotics, startup ideas).  
- **Academic Resources:** Well-equipped library with engineering references, online journals, and technical magazines.  

## Industry Collaboration & Placements  
CSE maintains strong industry ties. Faculty conduct workshops with IT experts, and students undertake industry projects. The curriculum is reviewed by industry leaders to ensure relevance. Key highlights:  
- **Conferences & Workshops:** Hosted IEEE conferences (FABS-2021 on big data and security, Comp-SIF 2025 on sustainable computing)【47†L641-L649】.  
- **Internships & Projects:** Students intern at leading tech companies (Nokia, Bosch, IBM, etc.) and collaborate on real-world software projects.  
- **Placements:** The placement cell reports excellent CSE outcomes – graduates secure jobs at top firms with competitive packages. In fact, the department notes “placements with highest salary”【47†L652-L656】. Major recruiters include Infosys, TCS, Wipro, IBM, Capgemini, Mindtree, and startups.  
- **Professional Training:** Faculty and students participate in NPTEL courses and industry certification programs (like IBM and Cisco training).  

## Achievements and Student Activities  
Students and faculty have earned numerous accolades. For example, CSE students won hackathons and paper awards, and faculty received AICTE LITE awards and IEEE Young Engineers recognitions【47†L648-L654】. The department publishes a newsletter and encourages participation in tech fests and coding competitions. A “Professor of Practice” program brings industry veterans to mentor students.
